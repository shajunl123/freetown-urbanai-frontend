# Climate Portfolio Brief

The Mayor's Office uses this pilot to retrieve approved evidence for briefing support.

## Portfolio Context

Freetown's climate portfolio brings together resilience planning, air quality work, drainage and flood adaptation, and partner-facing implementation updates.

## Briefing Use

This note is intended to help leadership teams prepare for donor and partner conversations with language grounded in approved project material.

## Claim Guardrail

External claims should be tied to cited programme documents, implementation notes, or approved briefing text rather than unsourced summaries.
